# Belgian Youth Chess Championships 2020

## From Sunday the 12th of April until Saterday the 18th of April 2020 in Floreal Blankenberge

As last year the youth championships are taking place in the Floreal in Blankenberge.

We welcome all youth players, parents, chess trainers and chess lovers during the 2nd week of Easter holidays and we are hoping for big crowds.

The championships are organized as in the previous years, by the npo Bycco.

Inscriptions and lodging reservations start at the 30th of November at 12h00
